1. Never share OTPs or passwords with anyone.
2. Always check the website URL before entering personal details.
3. Enable two-factor authentication on important accounts.
4. Avoid clicking suspicious links in emails or messages.
5. Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts.
6. Do not share banking details through phone calls or messages.
7. Regularly update your apps and software.
8. Be cautious when using public WiFi networks.

Financial/UPI Safety
• Always verify UPI payment requests before approving.
• Never scan unknown QR codes for receiving money.
